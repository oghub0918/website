YOU MUST WATCH THESE  ANIME BEFORE YOU DIE!
There are a lot of things to explore, books to read and especially anime to watch, and so if you can't decide which Anime you want to start watching, we at the OG HUB created the Top Five must watch anime of all time!

The medium of anime, like any other medium of story telling has a lot of varieties, from diffent genres, differeent perspective, animation style and an almost absurd number of series that it might be difficult to decide what to watch next! The series within this list is our best recommendation that we can give you if you ever wonder what great anime you can watch.

5. Steins;Gate
Steins;Gate is a SciFi anime that is based on a Visual novel  that follows the self proclaimed mad scienctist Hououin Kyouma, real name Okabe Rintarou, as he become entangled in affairs involving time travel when he saw a renowned scientist, Kurisu, die in front of him. After learning about time travel and traveling to a time before Kurisu's death, Kyouma, Kurisu and others created a time machine using an oven that can send messages back in time. Kyouma then learned that he changed something in the past, the present is affected and that other will have no memory of the last timeline except for him. 


4. Tengen Toppa Gurren Lagan
Gurren Lagan took place in a distant future where humans are forced to live in subterranean villages by the Spiral king, Lordgenome, who ruled the surface. He did it to protect humanity from a very powerful being from other space that can destroy the earth entirely. He ripped every human of their freedom but some wants to be free like Simon, a young boy who will drill through the heavens and set theirselver free!

3.Full Metal Alchemist
The story of Full Metal Alchemist occured in a universe where alchemy, which is the transformation of a substance to any substance with equal worth, is real. The story follows the brothers Edward and Alphonse Elric who after their father left them and their mother died, tried to resurrect her through alchemy, sacrificing Edwards two limb and Alphonse's body to give life to their mother, only to realize that they can't bring their mother from the dead and that she became a mindelss zombie after the resurrection. The brothers then set out to find the Philosophers Stone to regain their limbs and body.

2. Code Geass
 Code Geass focuses on Lelouch, a former prince of Britannia that was forced into hiding in Japan after Britannia's invation of Japan using giant mech robots. Lelouch now sets out to take revenge from his Father who abandonned him and his sister Nunnally to die in Japan. After being entangled in a terrorist attack in Japan, Lelouch meets C.C., a beautiful woman with a mysterious power. Lelouch then received this power called "Geass" which allowed him to control others. With this newfound strength, he sets out to revolt against Britannia.

1. Monogatari Series
Based on one of NISHIOISIN's work and his masterpiece, the Monogatari Series is a light novel/anime that follows the youth of Koyomi Araragi, a highschool who became a quasi vampire in his springbreak, dealth with a demon cat on Golden Week and caught a weightless girl at the start of the school year. It is filled with adventures and stories regarding the supernatural and also focuses on urban legends and has someheavy symbolism in every supernatural encounter.

Final Words

Deepen your love of anime with list top five list of the best must watch animes of all time. Discover many genres of anime from Scifi, supernatural, action, fantasy and more. We at OG HUB hopes that you will continue your exploration of the greater depths of Anime.
