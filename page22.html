TOP 5
Games of November 2019

Games has been a big part of the community since early 1950’s when academic computer scientists began designing simple games and simulation as part of their research. Since then a lot of games has been developed by different companies for different platforms. There are games for computer, consoles, and also for mobile phones.

Steam is one of the video game digital distribution service by Valve launched on September 2003. A lot of games is available to play on Steam either free to play or you need to pay for it from different genres of games, offline but most importantly online games. In this article we will take a look on the top 5 Most Played Steam Games for the past month:

5. Destiny 2
Having 73,879 players which totaled
66,922,429 hours played for the past month is the game developed by Bungie initially released for Playstation and Xbox on September 6, 2017 and released a pc version the following month.
Destiny 2 is an online-only multiplayer FPS video game which is set in a mythic science fiction world that features multiplayer environment with elements of role playing games.

 The game is divided into two which is the Player VS Environment and Player vs Player. Upon release, Destiny 2 received generally favorable reviews from critics. The game received praise for its many improvements over its predecessor. The original Destiny received much criticism for its story, but Destiny 2 was praised as having a "more robust story" and an actual fleshed out villain. Since its release, the game has been nominated in a lot of awards and won Best PC game, Best Online game and Best Multiplayer game in 2017.
.

4. Grant Theft Auto V
A game released in September 17, 2013 by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games it is the first main entry in the Grand Theft Auto series since 2008's Grand Theft Auto IV. The game is played for 54,856,046 hours in 1 month with 93,546 active players in Steam. GTA V is set in a fictional state called San Andreas, the single player story mode follows three criminals named Michael, Franklin, and Trevor as they finish heists with a lot of circumstances. 

The game offers an open world design which let you to play and do almost everything you want to do in the game and freely roam San Andreas and the city of Los Santos. GTA also offers an online multiplayer game where you can create your own gang with fellow players and build your own company. In my opinion, the GTA franchise is the best one to ever do it, it has a part on my childhood as I played their games from GTA San Andreas and others. Until now I’m still also waiting for the upcoming release of GTA VI since the last game is released for almost 7 years now.

3. DOTA 2
DOTA 2 has been played for 286,717,484 hours with 343,574 active players currently. DOTA 2 is a MOBA game or multiplayer online battle arena video game developed and also published by Valve. It is a sequel Defense of the Ancients, which was at first, only a community created mod for Warcraft III. 

DOTA 2 features a total of 117 playable heroes. Most of these are based off of pre-existing heroes from the first Defense of the Ancients. Some heroes such as Monkey King, Pangolier, Dark Willow, Grimstroke and Mars are entirely new heroes not found in the original version of DOTA, heroes are separated into groups according to their alignment, as well as their main attributes: Strength, Agility, and Intelligence. The game is running since 2009 and still very relevant today because of its good gameplay experience against other players. Now it also features arcade mode where you can play different games created by the Steam and DOTA community players also. DOTA 2 is a free to play game available on Steam since its release.

2. Conter Strike: Global Offensive
Currently having 381,444 current players and being played for 323,908,057 in a span of 30 days is the Counter Strike: Global Offensive, the game which is dubbed as the “game that never dies” because after being release on August 21, 2012 for PC, Xbox and Playstation 3, CSGO is still having hundreds of thousands of players playing and having a lot of tournaments since its release up to this date. It is a multiplayer FPS game developed by Valve and is the fourth game in the Counter Strike series. 

The game is an opposition of two teams, the Terrorist and the Counter-Terrorist group, and depends on what map you are playing on if the game is a bomb scenario situation or hostage situation just like the other Counter Strike games. Since the game has a lot of major and minor tournaments, it formed a lot of teams which is well-known to the CSGO Community such as the Astralis, NaVi, Cloud9 and many other teams which feature the greatest pros in Counter Strike history. In an update on 2018, Valve added a Battle Royale type game mode in CSGO called Dangerzone. One more reason that the game still attracts a lot of players is because of its continuous updates and new added features to the game. CSGO is available on Steam for free though it was a game you need to pay for before they made it free to play on late 2018. 

1. Player Unknown Battleground
And the number 1 game available on Steam for the past month is the PlayerUnknown’s Battleground or PUBG in short having played 229,609,276 by 405,244 current players. PUBG is released on March 23, 2017, it is an online multiplayer battle royale game developed and published by PUBG Corporation, a subsidiary of South Korean video game company Bluehole. 

The game battled Fortnite for the best battle royale game since the 2 games is released. PUBG is now also available on mobile for free while the PC version on Steam is a paid game with the price depending on the different promos the Steam market offers. PUBG has a great graphics, the main reason why it attracted a lot of players up to this date. The game also gained a lot of attention and became more popular because of Twitch streamers like Shroud and Dr Disrespect.
