TOP 5 Supernatural
anime

Explore the Bizzare World of Anime!
The world is filled with lots of weird stuff happening and odd events that can make you be filled by excitement or in fear of the unknown. We will now explore the great depths and mystery that this supernatural Animes can offer us!

From aliens, to mythologies to straight up horrors, the supernatural is filled with many wonders that is unknown to us humans that can bring us excitement. Today, the OG Hub presents a list of Top 5 supernatural animes that can either paralyzed with fear or to dive deeper to explore the wonders of the unknown.

5. Mononoke
Mononoke is a spin-off series of the horror anime titled as Ayakashi. It follows the protagonist of the 3rd part of Ayakashi, an unknown medicine seller who is very much involed with the supernatural and hunts down demons to prevent them from doing any more harm. The series is filled with oddities and mythologies from Japanese folklore. It is very much design to inflict fear to the viewer, from the attitude of the protagonist to the series overall artstyle.


4. Durarara
Not all supernatural events happens in the most hidden area of the world, some of them happens at the heart of the city, underneath the lamp of the city's nightlight. Durarara follows a young boy named Mikado who moves to Ikebukuro to study in high school and his adventure and mishaps as he delve deeper into the supernatural and criminal aspects of the city, from the infamous headless rider the "Black rider" and the mysterious organization named Dollars.

3.The Ancient Magus Bride
The Ancient Magus' Bride is a fantastical take to the supernatural aspects of the world. It follows the young woman named Chise who was sold as a slave in an auction. She was sold to a very tall gentleman who has a skull for his face and named Ainsworth. He is a magus that lived in solitary and taken Chise to be his apprentice. He trained her into the aspect of the supernatural in hopes that she will become his bride on day.

2. Mushishi
Mushishi is the most relaxing anime that you can watch. It has this calm atmosphere to it, unlike Mononoke, even though both characters deal with the supernatural and rarely talks, Ginko, the main character of Mushishi has a more calm atmosphere surrounding him. The series itself is also very relaxing as we Ginko in his adventures and dealing with issues regarding the Mushishis, though unlike the demons of Mononoker, are more akin to the spirit of nature.

1. Monogatari Series
Based on one of NISHIOISIN's work and his masterpiece, the Monogatari Series is a light novel/anime that follows the youth of Koyomi Araragi, a highschool who became a quasi vampire in his springbreak, dealth with a demon cat on Golden Week and caught a weightless girl at the start of the school year. It is filled with adventures and stories regarding the supernatural and also focuses on urban legends and has someheavy symbolism in every supernatural encounter.

Final Words

Do not fear the unknown, instead embrace it with open arms with these Supernatural Animes that can take you into a trip through the oddness that the world has. We hope that you can enjoy, be scared, be excitement, and be left wondering about the mystical after you watch the Animes in our recommendation list.
